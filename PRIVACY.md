# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

VyoraXR is a media browser and player. The app lets users configure their own local, network and online media sources.

## Data stored on the device

VyoraXR may store app settings, source addresses, bookmarks, authentication data, playback preferences and cached images on the user's device. Authentication data is used to connect to sources selected by the user.

## Network requests

The app sends requests directly to sources configured or selected by the user. Those services may receive technical information such as the user's IP address and device network details, subject to each service's own privacy policy.

## Developer data collection

VyoraXR does not intentionally send configured source credentials or private library information to the developer. This policy may be updated if optional diagnostics, accounts or other online services are introduced in a future release.

## Data control

Users can remove configured sources and clear the image cache within the app. Uninstalling the app removes its locally stored application data through Android's standard uninstall process.

## Contact

Privacy questions can be submitted through the GitHub Issues section without including credentials or private URLs.
